## Quiz Questions

### Q1: Pick your ancient civilization.
- 🛡️ Vikings — bold, fearless, and slightly chaotic → **Bold Adventurer**
- 🏺 Ancient Greece — philosophy, beauty, and olive oil → **Zen Minimalist**
- 🌸 Feudal Japan — ceremony, precision, and honor → **Artisan Snob**
- 🦅 Roman Empire — infrastructure, logistics, and getting things done → **Practical Pragmatist**

### Q2: It's Friday night. What's the move?
- 🏔️ Spontaneous road trip — figure out the rest later → **Bold Adventurer**
- 🍷 Wine, ambient music, and a book I've been meaning to finish → **Zen Minimalist**
- 📖 Dinner reservation at that new place everyone's talking about → **Artisan Snob**
- 🛋️ Couch, takeout, whatever's trending on Netflix → **Practical Pragmatist**

### Q3: Which Netflix show are you finishing in one sitting?
- 💥 Something with explosions, survival, and moral ambiguity → **Bold Adventurer**
- 🎭 A slow-burn drama with complex characters and no easy answers → **Zen Minimalist**
- 🌿 A documentary about artisanal food or obscure craftsmanship → **Artisan Snob**
- 😂 A comfort comedy I've already seen three times → **Practical Pragmatist**

### Q4: You have to move to a new city. How do you pick?
- 🌋 Somewhere wild and unexpected — the more unusual the better → **Bold Adventurer**
- ☕ Walkable, quiet, good light, good coffee shops → **Zen Minimalist**
- 🏯 Somewhere with deep history, culture, and great restaurants → **Artisan Snob**
- 🏙️ Good job market, reasonable cost of living, easy airport access → **Practical Pragmatist**

### Q5: You're building something. What's your approach?
- 🔨 Start immediately, figure it out as I go, embrace the chaos → **Bold Adventurer**
- 📐 Think deeply before touching anything. Measure twice, cut once → **Zen Minimalist**
- 🧘 Research obsessively, source the best materials, do it perfectly → **Artisan Snob**
- 📋 Follow the instructions, stay on schedule, hit the deadline → **Practical Pragmatist**
