## Quiz Questions

### Q1: It's Friday night. What's the move?
- 🏔️ Spontaneous road trip — figure it out as you go → **Bold Adventurer**
- 📖 Home, tea, a good book → **Zen Minimalist**
- 🍷 Dinner at that new place with the tasting menu → **Artisan Snob**
- 🛋️ Whatever's on, honestly just need to decompress → **Practical Pragmatist**

### Q2: Which Netflix show are you finishing in one sitting?
- 💥 Action thriller — explosions, twists, zero sleep → **Bold Adventurer**
- 🌿 Nature documentary — slow, beautiful, meditative → **Zen Minimalist**
- 🎭 Prestige drama — slow burn, lots to dissect afterward → **Artisan Snob**
- 😂 Comedy — easy, funny, no commitment → **Practical Pragmatist**

### Q3: You have to move to a new city. How do you pick?
- 🌋 Somewhere wild and adventurous — Tokyo, Buenos Aires → **Bold Adventurer**
- 🏯 Somewhere ancient and peaceful — Kyoto, Lisbon → **Zen Minimalist**
- ☕ Somewhere with an incredible food and coffee scene → **Artisan Snob**
- 🏙️ Wherever the job is — make it work → **Practical Pragmatist**

### Q4: Pick your ancient civilization:
- 🛡️ Vikings — raw power, fearless exploration → **Bold Adventurer**
- 🌸 Ancient Japan — discipline, beauty, restraint → **Zen Minimalist**
- 🏺 Ancient Greece — philosophy, art, refinement → **Artisan Snob**
- 🦅 Roman Empire — pragmatic, efficient, built to last → **Practical Pragmatist**

### Q5: You're building something. What's your approach?
- 🔨 Wing it and figure it out — done beats perfect → **Bold Adventurer**
- 🧘 Take your time, do it right, no shortcuts → **Zen Minimalist**
- 📐 Research extensively, source the best materials → **Artisan Snob**
- 📋 Follow the instructions, get it done → **Practical Pragmatist**
